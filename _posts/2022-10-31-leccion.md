## An introduction to JS to build a customized 404 page

You can easily serve custom 404 error pages with Jekyll to replace the default Error 404 – File Not Found page displayed when one tries to access a broken link on your site. Any 404.html at the root of your _site directory will be served automatically by GitHub Pages and the local WEBrick development server.

See the Jekyllrb documentation on [Custom 404 Page](https://jekyllrb.com/tutorials/custom-404-page/)

Para hacer una página 404 personalizada puede inspirarse en la que se describe en estos apuntes [404.md]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/temas/introduccion-a-javascript/jekyll#custom-404-page)
